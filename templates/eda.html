Data Insights & EDA

Dataset: 70,000 records from cardio_train.csv. Features include demographics, vitals, and lifestyle. Balanced target (50% cardio disease).

Key Insights:
  • Age: Mean ~53 years (range 30-65).
  • Gender: 65% men, 35% women.
  • Height/Weight: Mean height 164 cm, weight 74 kg.
  • Blood Pressure: Systolic mean 129 mmHg, Diastolic mean 97 mmHg (with outliers).
  • Cholesterol/Glucose: Mostly normal (means 1.37 and 1.23).
  • Lifestyle: 8.8% smokers, 5.4% alcohol consumers, 80.4% active.
Visualizations:

Boxplot: Blood Pressure (ap_hi ~120-130, ap_lo ~80, with high outliers).

Heatmap: Correlations (e.g., pulse_pressure & ap_hi: 0.84; cardio & age: 0.24).
